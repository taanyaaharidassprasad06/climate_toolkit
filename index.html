What is Climate Change?: Climate change is the long-term changes in Earth’s climate patterns caused by human activity such as burning fossil fuels which releases greenhouse gases into Earth’s atmosphere. The effects of climate change include rising temperatures, melting glaciers, droughts, storms, rising sea levels, etc. As a result of the several effects of climate change, climate change negatively impacts many of Earth’s precious wildlife and ecosystems.
